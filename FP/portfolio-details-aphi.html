Alpha Phi

Date: August 10, 2022

In this project, I wanted to create a website for my sorority, Alpha Phi, when I was the VP of Marketing. As a chapter, we've never had a website and our main source of sharing information was through social media like Instagram and Facebook. Since social media isn't a professional form of communication, I wanted to create a place that could be showcased in a professional setting. A lot of my sisters have mentioned how their parents didn't really know what sororities did, so I knew I had to make a page about philanthropy. I included more pages about the origins of our chapter along with who was currently on the executive board. Each page includes the options for viewers to ask individual questions. The website is hosted on wix.com.
