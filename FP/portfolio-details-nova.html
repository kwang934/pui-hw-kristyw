Nova

Date: April 3, 2023

In this project, our goal was to use conversational agents or voice assistants to benefit users in situations where their hands and/or their visual attention is not directly avaliable. We were assigned the domain, instructions for music, where we did primary and secondary research to find a problem that was worth solving. We found that there were 3 main pain points: picking up and putting down an instrument is a hassle, frustrating to repeatedly set a metronome, and rewinding a track is annoying.

Introducing Nova, a conversational user interface that helps violists annotate, record, and drill their practice sessions. Nova can record practice sessions, allow users to listen the playback, and deliver feedback. It can also help users drill down target problem areas with an integrated metronome. Lastly, the user can ask Nova to markup sheet music hands-free and receive reminders of annotations. Nova is most accessible on tablets as sheet music is paired.
