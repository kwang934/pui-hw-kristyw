PetPals

Date: December 12, 2022

In this project, our goal was to identify an unmet need in an assigned business domain: purchasing, adopting, or fostering a pet.We applied a series of research methods, such as going to the local Pittsburgh animal shelter and interviewed the employees about their day-to-day challenges. This research along with many storyboards and iterations led us to design a social media platform for animal shelter employees to communicate.

The homepage features a place where people could ask questions and get them answered by professionals. We included upcoming collaborations to build value for service providers and owners that wanted to be reminded of their tasks. The chat page uses implicit knowledge to chat with other professionals. We wanted to include a calendar in the top right to allow users to connect with the other party in-person through scheduling a meeting. The last photo showcases a animal shelter employees' contacts, questions, replies, and experience.
