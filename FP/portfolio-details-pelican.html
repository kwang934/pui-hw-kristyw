Pelican Sport

Date: February 24, 2023

In this project, we were assigned the company, Pelican Sport, to help them find new sources of value and introduce a new way of thinking withirn the company. The limitations of this project were to take advantage of smartphone capabilities and use emerging technology. We decided to focus on the kayak catergory of Pelican Sport along with using AI to generate potential kayaking routes.

Our hypothesis was that "if Pelican can provide two-fold care to kayakers by providing adventure and to partners through the opportunity to donate, then Pelican can gain greater insight on what customers care about and improve their business decisions." The user flow starts out on a page which offers the user a kayaking trail to explore. Users are able to use stamps to share their accomplishments and alert their friends of events they're going to. By completing trails, users earn stars, which they can use to donate to an organization of their choice. Users will again make their mark through using their stamp and add to the value of giving back. The kayaker's profile provides access to various features such as kayak stats, completed challenges, maps, and friends. Users can view a collective map that includes both their own and their friends' adventures after sharing their own experiences. This feature encourages users to connect and share their experiences with one another, fostering a sense of community among kayakers.
